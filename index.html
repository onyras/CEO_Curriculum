Konstantin Method Curriculum

A Map for the Ongoing Work of Leadership

The person with the fewest blind spots wins.

This isn't a program you complete. It's a map I've developed over years of working with founders and CEOs who already know how to run a company. They don't need the basics. They need someone to help them see what they've been avoiding.

My growing library consists of 60+ frameworks, guides, and deep-dives across three domains: Leading Yourself, Leading Others, and Leading the Organization.

Most leadership development gets this backwards. It starts with strategy, structure, and systems. But organizations are just people coordinating. And people can only coordinate as well as they can see themselves and each other.

Clarity precedes effectiveness. Always.

The Three Territories

The method covers three territories, in this order

Leading Yourself

The inner work that makes outer work possible

Leading Others

Building trust and connection at scale

Leading the Organization

Shaping systems that outlast you

Every Territory Has 2 Tracks To Choose From

  • Foundations: The essential disciplines. Even experienced leaders have gaps here.
  • Advanced: The harder questions. Where seasoned CEOs often find the real work.

We won't go through this sequentially. Based on where you are and what you're facing, we'll pull what's relevant. Think of it as a map, not a syllabus.
